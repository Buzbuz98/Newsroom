VAT Refund System Discontinued for European Businesses: Find Out Which Countries are Affected

The VAT refund system for certain European countries has been discontinued causing disruption and confusion for affected businesses. An online portal has been created to help navigate this change, providing information on affected countries and alternative options for tax compliance.
